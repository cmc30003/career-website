Higher Education Data Consulting

I help colleges, universities, and schools build reliable data systems, modernize reporting processes, and develop the data capacity their teams need to make informed decisions with confidence.

My consulting work draws from experience as a Director of Data Management and Lead Data Warehouse Developer at research universities, combined with graduate-level training in learning, leadership, and organizational development. I work with institutions navigating complexity, building toward sustainability, or trying to get more value from their existing data infrastructure.

How I Can Help

Data Infrastructure Assessment

I evaluate your current data systems, identify gaps in reporting pipelines, and develop a practical roadmap for improvement. This includes reviewing data sources, workflows, tooling, and documentation to understand what is working, what is fragile, and what is missing.

Assessment Strategy Roadmapping

Reporting Modernization

I design and build Power BI dashboards, SQL reporting pipelines, and automated workflows that replace manual processes and reduce institutional reliance on one-time exports and spreadsheet-based reporting. I focus on solutions that are maintainable, documented, and built for the people who will use them.

Power BI SQL Automation

Institutional Research Support

I assist institutional research offices with IPEDS reporting, enrollment analysis, accreditation data preparation, and analytics infrastructure. Whether you need targeted support during a transition or structured help building long-term reporting capacity, I can step in with experience and context.

IPEDS Enrollment Accreditation

ETL Development & Pipeline Design

I build and optimize data pipelines that connect student information systems, financial systems, HR systems, and reporting platforms. My ETL work prioritizes reliability, transparency, and sustainability so your team is not dependent on a process no one understands.

ETL Data Pipelines Integration

Data Governance & Documentation

I develop data dictionaries, governance frameworks, and documentation practices that improve data quality, reduce confusion, and build institutional trust in reported information. Good governance is not just policy — it is shared understanding.

Governance Documentation Data Quality

Data Literacy & Staff Development

I design training programs and workshops to help staff interpret, use, and communicate with data more confidently. Building data literacy within your institution reduces dependency on technical specialists and creates a more engaged, data-informed organizational culture.

Training Data Literacy Capacity Building
